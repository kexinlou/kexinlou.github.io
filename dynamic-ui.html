Reimagine Modern Productivity Tools

Overview:

This is a passion project that I collaborated with two other designers for Microsoft’s annual Hackathon - Fix, Hack, Learn.

Problem:

There’re 10+ apps in Microsoft 365 suite and it is untenable for consumers to fully learn how to operate all apps and make the most out of their subscription.

Solution:

We want to bring Microsoft products to our customers instead. We combined different app capabilities and command into a canvas where each element on the canvas has a simple and contextual control that help customers reach their goals where they are.
